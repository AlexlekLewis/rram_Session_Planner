# BRAND & VISUAL IDENTITY GUIDELINES SOP: RAJASTHAN ROYALS

## 1. Master Brand Narrative & Core Values
* **Operating Philosophy**: Brand drives performance and defines the underlying spirit governing all on-field and off-field actions.
* **Narrative**: Royalty is earned through courage, undying spirit, respect, and willingness to go above and beyond.
* **Core Values**:
    * **Always Fan-First**: Keep customers at the center of everything.
    * **Exhibit Resilience**: Adapt and bounce back with a positive mindset.
    * **Stay Curious**: Create an environment to learn, un-learn, and challenge the status quo.
    * **Have Empathy**: Inclusive and diverse environment where people feel valued.
    * **Data-Driven Mindset**: Use data to guide decision-making and validate action.
    * **Display Integrity**: Always do the right thing regardless of consequences.

## 2. Logo Hierarchy & Technical Standards (Law of the Crest and Seal)
### The Royal Crest (Primary)
* **Priority**: Prioritize in all communications except television.
* **Usage**: Must always be used as a unit.
* **Background Laws**:
    * **On White**: Use in pink or blue.
    * **On Black**: MUST be white.
    * **On Gradient**: MUST be white.

### The Royal Seal (Secondary)
* **Constraint**: ONLY use in communications pertaining to television.
* **Usage**: Must always be used as a unit.

### Global Logo Constraints (The "Don'ts")
* **Prohibited**: NEVER re-arrange, re-size, or re-color internal elements.
* **Prohibited**: NEVER outline elements, use special effects, rotate, or distort the symbol.
* **Prohibited**: NEVER place within an unapproved box or shape.

### Sizing & Clear Space Law
* **Clear Space**: Minimum space is the width of the **'R'** from 'Royals' on all sides.
* **Minimum Width (Print)**: 25mm.
* **Minimum Width (Web)**: 70px.

## 4. Typography Protocol
### Header Law (Romagna Family)
* **Romagna Exo**: ONLY for short headers (2-3 words), highlights, and isolated numbers.
* **Romagna**: For longer headers and official slogans.
* **EXO FORBIDDEN USAGE**:
    * NEVER use for entity names (e.g., Rajasthan Royals).
    * NEVER use for "Halla Bol" (must use simple Romagna).
    * NEVER use in body copy or sentences ≥ 4 words.
    * NEVER use on print, TV, hoardings, merchandise, or PPTs.

### Body Text & Presentations
* **Montserrat**: Standard for all text bodies and small-scale elements (digital & physical).
* **Verdana**: EXCLUSIVE for PowerPoint presentations (headers, body, charts).

## 5. Visual Assets & Photography
### The Arrow Asset (Key Visual)
* **Evolution**: Evolved from the logo crown to form a bespoke design language.
* **Usage Limits**: NO MORE than three arrows per creative.
* **Width Standard**: Arrow width MUST be identical within a single creative.
* **Gradient Law**: Gradients MUST be manually managed to signify progress and movement.

### Iconography & Photography
* **Icons**: Derived from the arrow; MUST feature curved edges and single strokes in one color.
* **Photography**: Focus on athletic body language and forceful movements.
* **Editing**: Photographs should be desaturated, textured, and highlighted with pink and blue tones.

## 6. Sub-Brand Entities
### Royal Rajasthan Foundation
* **Color Usage**: Primarily Blue & Gold on white; can use Pink.
* **Clear Space**: Scale the jewel in the logo by **200%** to calculate minimum space.
* **Secondary Palette**: RF Pink (Pantone 218 C) and RA Blue (Pantone 3005 C).

### Royals School of Business (RSB)
* **Minimum Width (Print)**: 50mm.
* **Minimum Width (Web)**: 140px.
